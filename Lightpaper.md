# AIGEN Lightpaper

## Revolutionizing AI with Blockchain Technology

### Executive Summary

AIGEN is a groundbreaking blockchain platform that integrates artificial intelligence capabilities directly into the blockchain infrastructure. By combining decentralized consensus with AI model execution, AIGEN creates a new paradigm for trustworthy, transparent, and economically efficient AI services.

### The Problem

Current AI ecosystems suffer from:
- **Centralization**: Control concentrated in tech giants
- **Lack of Transparency**: Black-box decision making
- **High Costs**: Prohibitive infrastructure expenses
- **Trust Issues**: No verifiable audit trails
- **Monetization Barriers**: Difficult for AI developers to earn fairly

### Our Solution

AIGEN introduces **Proof-of-Intelligence (PoI)**, a novel consensus mechanism where network participants contribute computational resources for AI model execution while securing the blockchain.

### Token Economics

#### AIGEN Token Utility
- **Network Fees**: Pay for AI inference and transactions
- **Staking**: Secure the network and earn rewards
- **Governance**: Vote on protocol upgrades and model additions
- **Subscription Payment**: Access premium AI models and features

#### Tier System
- **Free**: 10 requests/month, basic models, ads supported
- **Basic**: 100 requests/month, 50 AIGEN/month, no ads
- **Pro**: 1000 requests/month, 400 AIGEN/month, all models
- **Unlimited**: Pay-per-use (1 AIGEN/1K tokens), volume discounts
